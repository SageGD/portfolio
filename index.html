Cyanotype Postcard Project

Process Design Instructions Photography

Designed comprehensive process documentation for creating botanical art using traditional cyanotype printing techniques. This project combines historical photographic processes with modern instructional design to make the art form accessible to beginners.

Process Steps

  • Treat postcard with cyanotype solution and allow to dry
  • Collect and arrange dried flowers, ferns, and leaves
  • Add wooden letter typography for personalization
  • Expose under glass in sunlight for 8 minutes
  • Wash in clean water until clear for final blue development

Design Features

  • Clear step-by-step instructions with visual hierarchy
  • Safety and timing guidance for consistent results
  • Integration of typography and natural materials
  • Accessible format for makers of all skill levels
